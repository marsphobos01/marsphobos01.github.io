Welcome — why I'm writing this blog

Short first post. I build a lot of things — for clients, for uni, and mostly just because an idea won't leave me alone — and the interesting parts almost never make it into a portfolio card. This is where those go.

What you'll find here

  • Build logs — how a project actually came together, including the bits that broke.
  • Lessons — things I got wrong once so (hopefully) I don't get them wrong twice.
  • Deep-dives — the occasional rabbit hole I fell down and decided to write up.

Most of it will sit at the intersection of the things I care about: secure, well-built software, networking and systems, and — almost always — music. If any of that is your thing too, stick around.

— Morgan
